- Data: Open-Meteo API, Real-time collection, Historical storage

## 📞 FAQ

### Q: Why split Phase 4 this way?
A: Each feature has clear separation:
- Layers are independent (weather, AQI, wind, etc)
- Timeline is completely separate
- Controls are reusable

### Q: How long will Phase 4 take?
A: 3-5 working days (23 hours estimated)
- 1 day: foundation + temperature layer
- 1 day: AQI layer + heatmaps
- 1 day: timeline slider
- 1 day: controls + search + analytics
- 0.5 day: testing + optimization

### Q: Can I skip Phase 5?
A: Phase 4 alone reaches 85-90% completion.
Phase 5 takes it to 90-100% (polish, scale, users).

### Q: What if I run into issues?
A: Fallback strategy:
- No timeline? Show single time point (still impressive with layers)
- No heatmaps? Keep markers with layer colors (still professional)
- No search? Keep map controls (still functional)
